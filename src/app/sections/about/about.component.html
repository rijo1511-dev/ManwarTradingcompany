About Manawer Trading & Contracting

Since opening our doors in 1999, Manawer Trading & Contracting has grown from a small local builder into one of Qatar’s respected, employee-owned construction and maintenance partners. Over the years, we have built our reputation on integrity, quality workmanship, timely delivery, and a personalized approach to every project we undertake.

Our steady growth is the result of strong client relationships and our commitment to understanding each project’s unique needs. Today, we proudly serve both government and private sector clients, many of whom continue to return to us because they trust our reliability and performance.

Our Experience

With over 25 years of industry experience, we have successfully completed 1,000+ projects across Qatar in the industrial, commercial, institutional, retail, and residential sectors.
